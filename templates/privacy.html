Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly and a small amount of technical information needed to operate the service securely.

Specifically, we collect:

  • Account information: your email address and a securely hashed password used to authenticate you.
  • Expense records: the transactions, categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions you choose to log within the app.
  • Usage data: basic information such as pages visited, features used, and error logs, used to keep the service reliable and improve it over time.
  • Device and technical data: browser type, IP address, and timestamps of access, used for security and diagnostics.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, payment card details, or financial account credentials. Spendly is a tracking tool — it never asks for your bank login.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect only to operate, secure, and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • Store, retrieve, and display the expense records you create.
  • Generate the dashboards, summaries, and reports you request within the app.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to abuse, fraud, and security incidents.
  • Communicate with you about service changes, security notices, or support requests.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not use your expense data for advertising, and we do not share it with data brokers.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers operated by trusted infrastructure providers. We apply industry-standard safeguards — encryption in transit (HTTPS), encryption of sensitive fields at rest, and access controls limited to personnel who need it to operate the service.

Passwords are hashed using a modern, salted hashing algorithm — Spendly staff cannot read your password. Expense records are associated with your account and are not visible to other users.

You may export your data and you may delete your account at any time from within the app. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed from active systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law (for example, basic accounting records we may be obligated to keep).

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is perfectly secure. If we ever become aware of a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

4. Third Party Services

To run Spendly, we rely on a small number of carefully chosen third-party providers. These providers process data on our behalf only as needed to deliver the service and are bound by confidentiality and security obligations.

  • Hosting and infrastructure: a reputable cloud provider that stores database records and serves the application.
  • Email delivery: a transactional email provider used for account verification, password resets, and important service notices.
  • Analytics: lightweight, privacy-respecting analytics that help us understand feature usage. Where used, analytics are anonymized or aggregated.

We do not integrate advertising networks, social media trackers, or advertising SDKs. If a future feature requires sharing data with an additional third party, we will update this policy and notify active users before the change takes effect.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You are in control of your data. Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal information we hold about you.

  • Access: you can view all of your expense data from within the app at any time.
  • Export: you can download your records in a portable format.
  • Correction: you can edit any expense entry directly within the app.
  • Deletion: you can delete individual entries or close your account entirely.

If you have a request that cannot be handled from within the app, please reach out through the contact channels below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, the Service, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you — for example, by posting a notice within the Service or by sending an email to the address associated with your account.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the latest revisions took effect. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the support channels provided within the Service. We take privacy questions seriously and will do our best to respond in a timely and transparent manner.
